Odin Recipe - Nachos


These homemade nachos are great for game day. This recipe makes a huge meal-sized tray of nachos loaded with taco-seasoned ground beef, refried beans, salsa, jalape√±os, and cheese.


Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning Mix
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Refried Beans
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook ground beef, until no longer pink
  3. Add water and taco seasoning mix
  4. Spread tortilla chips on baking sheet
  5. Top with cheddar cheese, refried beans and ground beed mixture
  6. Broil in a preheated oven until cheese is melted
  7. Top with salsa and sour cream
