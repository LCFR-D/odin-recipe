Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place warm water in bowl, add yest and sugar, mix
  3. Add flour, oil and salt, beat until smooth.
  4. Rest for 5 Minutes
  5. Turn dough onto floured surface and pat or roll into a circle
  6. Transfer into a prepared pizza pan
  7. Spread crust with sauce and toppings
  8. Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown
